# Art Direction

## Direction

Commercial-grade target: Santa Monica Studio atmosphere, Assassin's Creed scale, Apple-level restraint, and healthcare analytics subject matter translated into mythic architecture.

## Visual Language

- Massive stone forms
- Copper, marble, black granite, white ivory, blue energy
- No generic fantasy parchment
- No Bootstrap cards
- UI behaves like carved mechanisms and illuminated plates
- Data appears as rivers, runes, crystals, aqueducts, and celestial diagrams

## Palette

- Archive: ivory, amber, dust, old bronze
- Forge: charcoal, copper, fire orange, electric cyan
- Citadel: white marble, navy banners, gold trim
- Sanctum: black stone, blue volumetric light
- Observatory: dark sky, silver, nebula violet
- Workshop: steel, leather, brass, living green light

## Sound Direction

Sprint 1 includes procedural browser audio instead of external music files. Sprint 2 should replace this with adaptive stems: exploration, discovery, puzzle, vault, storm, and victory.

## Artwork Pipeline

Sprint 1 uses procedural canvas art so there are no placeholder images. Sprint 2 should add generated/commissioned hero assets while preserving the established visual language.
